Registration


The Marathon is open to teams that comprise up to three (3) student members, who must be undergraduate or graduate students, and a coach. If a team wants to compete for the prize, all the competitors must attend SSCAD conference and register for the local contest - see the conference portal at https://sscad2025.ufms.br
